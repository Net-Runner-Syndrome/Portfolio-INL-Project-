Shaping the Next Generation of Developers

Dr. Alex Morgan is a passionate Senior Lecturer at Belgium Campus iTversity, specialising in software engineering, innovative leadership, and full-stack development. With over eight years of teaching experience, Dr. Morgan bridges the gap between academic theory and real-world application.

Known for an energetic teaching style, Dr. Morgan believes that every student has the potential to become a world-class developer — they just need the right environment, the right challenge, and someone who believes in them.

"Technology is just a tool. My job is to help you think like an engineer — to break any problem down until it's solvable, and then to build something the world hasn't seen before."
